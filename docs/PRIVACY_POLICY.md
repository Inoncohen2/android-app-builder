# Privacy Policy Guide

## Required for Google Play

Google Play requires ALL apps to have a Privacy Policy URL.

## How to create one:

### Option 1: Use Free Generator
1. Go to https://app-privacy-policy-generator.firebaseapp.com/
2. Fill in your app details
3. Generate policy
4. Host on your website
5. Enter URL in Web2App build form

### Option 2: Use Your Website
If your website has a privacy policy, use that URL.

### Option 3: GitHub Pages (Free Hosting)
1. Create `privacy.html` in your GitHub repo
2. Enable GitHub Pages
3. Use URL: https://yourusername.github.io/yourrepo/privacy.html

## What to include:

Your privacy policy must explain:
- What data you collect
- How you use it
- How users can request deletion
- Your contact information
